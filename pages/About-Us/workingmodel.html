WORKING MODEL

SFC is engaged in various Social and Community Development programs aimed for the benefit of poor, vulnerable and needy women, senior citizens, children of our society. Our objective is not only to eradicate illiteracy among the weaker sections of the society but also to uplift them and levy the foundation for Women Empowerment, Healthcare, Development and Human rights.

Our core philosophy has its bedrock in education as we believe it is the only weapon which can change the world and the best way to make this grand change is to impart education to the building block of our society i.e. our children. Hence, we ensure that each child gets access to these opportunities. However, the complete eradication of illiteracy will happen only by educating the women and elder folks, along with children. So, we have devoted special Programs for all of them. Our Vision and Mission are fulfilled wholeheartedly by our utmost core values, intense dedication and commitment of our Team.

But as we all know Vision alone is not enough, so combining it with the venture, we have our three main projects- Project Gyan, Project Sampuran and Project Vatavarn which are the hearts of our Working Model. The education imparted to the lesser fortunate is covered under Project Gyan. And all the other crucial issues of the society are dealt under Project Sampuran like visiting Old Age Homes, or organizing Donation Drives or Awareness Campaigns. Project Vatavarn deals with all the issues related to Environmental Protection and Animal Welfare. And to conquer huge development challenges, we have a colossal pool of Interns and Volunteers, who are pivotal members of our Foundation. When like-minded people come together with common aspirations, great results can be achieved and SFC has become the platform of success due to such devoted people only.
