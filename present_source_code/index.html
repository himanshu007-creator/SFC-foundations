Write the reality campaign

SELF DEFENSE WEBINAR honoured by Fast Media

Fast Media, a renowned Punjabi newspaper honoured three-days training programme by SFC Foundations on Self-Defense in collaboration with Chandigarh Police to make the youth realize the importance of Self Protection in highly uncertain times, held from 22nd to 24th October, 2020 via Zoom app, under the guidance of Mr Sanjay, Incharge of Swayam Team Chandigarh.
The consequent days were full of practical experience, consisting of exercises, defense & attack techniques and hacks, griping & holding techniques in Martial Arts and Karate, awareness about safety tools like "pepper spray and knife" , etc.

NOOR-E-DIWALI

A campaign initiated by SFC Foundations , marked it's beginning on the eve of Deepawali. This campaign aimed at giving a ray of hope and help in respect of employment and earning to Smt. Kamlesh , an old artisan and resident of Sector- 25, Chandigarh and celebrating Eco-friendly Deepawali by lighting up Cowdung Diyas. This campaign marked it's ending with a huge success by giving an order of 1000 diyas to Smt. Kamlesh Devi and cash Rs. 5000 for her efforts, love and dedication in making Cowdung Diyas.

'Baapu Ke Nakshe Kadam' admired by Fast Media

At the end of the day, everyone was filled with positivity and hope and more inspired by the way of living of Gandhi Ji.

Feeding Paws honoured by Times of India

On 25 September, 2020 , Times of India recognised and honoured SFC Foundations' campaign ' Feeding Paws' under it's E- paper.

It appreciated the efforts of SFC team members to launch a new campaign Feeding Paws with a unique plot of adopting a stray animal and feeding it like ones own pet during coronavirus lockdown that has also forced the four legged strays in extreme distress as lack of food and water are impacting their lives gravely.This campaign has become successful and team has been feeding over 500+ cats and dogs since the lockdown across Delhi, Chandigarh, Rajpura, Panchkula and Jalandhar.
